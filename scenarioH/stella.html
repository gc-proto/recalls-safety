Stella Artois Beer - Presence of glass particles

Stella Artois

Recall date: April 2, 2018

Summary

  • Product: Stella Artois
  • Issue: Presence of glass particles in certain lots
  • What to do: Throw out affected products

Affected products

Check the best before date on your bottle, and then look at the time stamp. The time stamp is in a 24-hour format (09:00 means 9:00 AM, and 21:00 means 9:00 PM).

Issue

Labatt Brewing Company Limited is recalling Stella Artois brand Beer from the marketplace due to possible presence of glass particles. Consumers should not consume the affected products.

Only Stella Artois brand Beer sold in glass bottles is affected by this recall.

What you should do

Check to see if you have the affected product. Recalled products should be thrown out. Other lots of Stella Artois beer are safe.

Background

This recall was triggered by the company. The Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA) is conducting a food safety investigation, which may lead to the recall of other products. If other high-risk products are recalled, the CFIA will notify the public through updated Food Recall Warnings.

The CFIA is verifying that industry is removing recalled product from the marketplace.
